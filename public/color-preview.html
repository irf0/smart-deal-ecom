Current vs proposed

Current — warm beige
Proposed — clean premium
Recommendation: Bright blue (#2563EB) feels more modern and tech-forward — good for a marketplace positioning. Navy (#1E3A5F) reads more premium/banking — better if you want a "certified & secure" tone. Orange CTAs (#D4532A) work with both; keep them for buttons and urgency, not hero backgrounds. The warm beige neumorphic look shifts to a cooler, flatter premium aesthetic with subtler shadows.
